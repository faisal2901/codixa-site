About Codixa

We build the future, one ship at a time

Codixa is a design and development studio on a mission to make world-class software affordable for every business. From a kirana store in Lucknow to a startup in San Francisco.

Founder's note

Hi, I'm Mohammed Faisal

I started Codixa because great software shouldn't cost a fortune. Traditional agencies charge premium rates for inflated timelines, account managers, and big offices. We run lean: a sharp team, low overheads, and direct client communication. That means you get the same quality at a fraction of the price.

We work as one team of engineers, designers, and project leads, with direct communication between you and the people actually building your product. No middlemen, no junior handoffs. The result: faster delivery, fair prices, software that genuinely works.

We work with founders in India and across the globe: D2C brands, SaaS startups, solo creators, and professional services firms. Anyone who needs software shipped without the agency tax.
