About Codixa

We build the future, one ship at a time

Codixa is an AI-native software studio on a mission to make world-class software affordable for every business. From a kirana store in Lucknow to a startup in San Francisco.

Founder's note

Hi, I'm Mohammed Faisal

I started Codixa because I saw a gap that didn't exist 18 months ago. AI has fundamentally changed how software gets built. What used to require a 5-person team and a 3-month timeline now takes one person and 3 weeks. But most agencies haven't restructured around this. They're still charging 2022 prices for 2022 timelines.

Codixa is built natively for this new era. We use AI as our engineering team, design partner, and ops support, while a human (me) handles strategy, quality, and the client relationship. The result: faster delivery, lower prices, better product.

We work with founders in India and across the globe: D2C brands, SaaS startups, solo creators, and professional services firms. Anyone who needs software shipped without the agency tax.
