Pricing FAQ

Questions about pricing

Why are your rates lower than typical agencies? +

We run a lean, in-house team without the overhead of large offices, account-manager hierarchies, or layered approval chains. That keeps our cost base low, so we can charge significantly less while delivering work of the same quality.

How do I know the price before I commit? +

Share your project details with us via the Contact form or WhatsApp. Within 4 hours, we send back a detailed quote covering scope, timeline, deliverables, and the final fixed price. No surprises after that, no hidden fees.

What's your payment structure? +

50% upfront to start work, 50% on delivery. For larger projects, we offer milestone-based payments spread over the build. Indian clients pay via UPI, bank transfer, or Razorpay. Global clients pay via Stripe or Wise.

What if I'm not happy with the result? +

Every package includes revisions. If after revisions you're still unhappy and we haven't delivered what was agreed, you get a partial refund proportional to incomplete work. We stand behind our craft.

Do you provide GST invoices? +

Yes. Indian clients get GST-compliant invoices. Global clients get formal invoices suitable for accounting and tax purposes in their country.

Can I upgrade or scale up later? +

Absolutely. Many clients start small and expand once they see results. We credit your previous spend toward the next package and keep the same team on your project for continuity.
