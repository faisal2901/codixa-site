Pricing FAQ

Questions about pricing

Why are your prices so much lower than other agencies? +

We use AI-native workflows that let one skilled engineer deliver what used to require a 5-person team. We pass those savings to you. Quality stays the same (or better) because every output gets human review.

Are these really fixed prices? No hidden fees? +

Yes. The price you see is the price you pay. The only extras are services you explicitly add (like AI Boost or Care plans). Domain, hosting, and third-party tools (Stripe, etc.) are paid directly by you.

What's your payment structure? +

50% upfront to start work, 50% on delivery. For larger projects (above ₹1,00,000 / $3,000) we offer milestone-based payments. Indian clients pay via UPI, bank transfer, or Razorpay. Global clients pay via Stripe or Wise.

What if I'm not happy with the result? +

Every package includes revisions. If after revisions you're still unhappy and we haven't delivered what was agreed, you get a partial refund proportional to incomplete work. We've never had to do this. But the guarantee exists.

Do you provide GST invoices? +

Yes. Indian clients get GST-compliant invoices. Global clients get formal invoices suitable for accounting and tax purposes in their country.

Can I upgrade between packages? +

Absolutely. Many clients start with Spark or Presence, then upgrade to Launch when they want to add functionality. We credit your previous spend toward the new package.
